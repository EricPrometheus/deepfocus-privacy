DeepFocus 隐私政策

最后更新：2025 年 3 月 20 日

概述

DeepFocus（"我们"）非常重视您的隐私。本隐私政策说明我们在您使用 DeepFocus 移动应用（"应用"）时如何处理您的信息。

数据收集

我们不收集任何数据。

DeepFocus 是一款完全本地运行的应用。您的所有数据（包括钓鱼记录、收藏品、设置和统计信息）仅存储在您的设备本地。我们不会收集、存储、传输或共享任何个人信息。

具体说明

  • 我们不要求用户注册或创建账户
  • 我们不收集个人身份信息（姓名、邮箱、电话号码等）
  • 我们不收集设备信息或位置信息
  • 我们不使用 Cookie 或类似的跟踪技术
  • 我们不接入任何第三方分析工具或广告 SDK
  • 应用不包含任何广告
  • 应用不需要网络连接即可使用

本地存储

应用使用设备的本地存储（Hive 数据库）保存您的游戏进度和设置。这些数据完全由您控制，您可以随时通过卸载应用来删除所有数据。

第三方服务

DeepFocus 不使用任何第三方服务。

儿童隐私

DeepFocus 适合所有年龄段的用户。由于我们不收集任何数据，因此不存在儿童个人信息泄露的风险。

政策变更

如果我们更新本隐私政策，将在此页面更新"最后更新"日期。建议您定期查看本政策。

DeepFocus Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 20, 2025

Overview

DeepFocus ("we") respects your privacy. This privacy policy explains how we handle your information when you use the DeepFocus mobile application ("App").

Data Collection

We do not collect any data.

DeepFocus is a fully offline application. All your data — including fishing records, collections, settings, and statistics — is stored exclusively on your device. We do not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal information.

Details

  • We do not require user registration or account creation
  • We do not collect personally identifiable information (name, email, phone number, etc.)
  • We do not collect device information or location data
  • We do not use cookies or similar tracking technologies
  • We do not integrate any third-party analytics or advertising SDKs
  • The App contains no advertisements
  • The App does not require an internet connection to function

Local Storage

The App uses your device's local storage (Hive database) to save your game progress and settings. This data is entirely under your control, and you can delete all data by uninstalling the App.

Third-Party Services

DeepFocus does not use any third-party services.

Children's Privacy

DeepFocus is suitable for users of all ages. Since we do not collect any data, there is no risk of children's personal information being compromised.

Changes to This Policy

If we update this privacy policy, we will update the "Last updated" date on this page. We recommend reviewing this policy periodically.
